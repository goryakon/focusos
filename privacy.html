FocusOS Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

1. Overview

FocusOS ("we", "the app") is an AI-powered personal productivity planner. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account info — your name, email address, and profile picture from Google or GitHub OAuth login (via Supabase Auth).
  • Task data — tasks, notes, steps, attachments, and energy levels you create inside the app. Stored in your personal Supabase account row, keyed to your user ID.
  • Google Calendar data — if you connect Google Calendar, we read and write events in a dedicated "FocusOS" calendar only. We do not access any other calendars. Your Google OAuth token is stored locally in your browser (localStorage) and never sent to our servers.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To sync your tasks across your devices.
  • To show your Google Calendar events alongside your tasks (Today and Backlog views).
  • To create, edit, and delete calendar events you explicitly request.
  • We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising.

4. Google Calendar Access

FocusOS uses the Google Calendar API with the scope https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar. This allows the app to:

  • Create and manage a dedicated "FocusOS" calendar in your Google account.
  • Read events from that calendar to display them in the app.
  • Create, edit, and delete events that you add through FocusOS.

We do not read, modify, or delete events from any of your other calendars. Your access token is stored only in your browser's localStorage and expires automatically. You can disconnect at any time by clicking the 📅 calendar icon and selecting "Disconnect".

FocusOS's use of Google user data complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Task data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL), protected by row-level security — only you can access your data.
  • Authentication is handled by Supabase Auth (OAuth via Google or GitHub).
  • Google OAuth tokens are stored only in your browser's localStorage — never on our servers.
  • The app is served over HTTPS at all times.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Google — OAuth login and Calendar API
  • GitHub — optional OAuth login
  • Anthropic — AI features (Brain Dump analysis, task breakdown)
  • Vercel — hosting

7. Data Retention & Deletion

Your task data is retained as long as you have an account. To delete all your data, sign out and contact us — we will permanently delete your record from the database within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

FocusOS is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest revision. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
